Hockey is an Amazing Sport and it is Lots of Fun for Various Reasons.

  1. It is a fast paced game where players fly down the ice at remarkable speeds.
  2. There is a lot of physical contact that includs checking and fighting. Yeah people can fight.
  3. It isn't like soccer where people get sent to the ER because they got patted on the back (that literaly happens) but hockey players actually get up and shake it off and just play the game, one time a players throat got cut open from a skate and he didn't just lie down he acually got up and left the rink to get taken care of. And after he recovered he got back on the ice!
  4. If you want to win a hockey game you have to have teamwork, you have to pass the puck to teammates because if you just try to score by yourself trust me, it won't happen.
